BEATS is one of the biggest, entrepreneurial, zestful, cultural, vibrant, dynamic, and energetic annual fests of the National Institute of Technology Delhi, conducted by the Startup centre for Innovation and Entrepreneurship (SCIE) and coordinated by other clubs of NIT Delhi.
